## Patterns of the Top 10% (What Winners Do)

### Pattern 1: Crystal-Clear Value Proposition Above the Fold
The best sites communicate WHAT they do, WHO it's for, and WHY them — in under 8 seconds. AmiSa's current site is JS-rendered which delays this. The new site should have an immediately visible, compelling headline.

### Pattern 2: Process / How It Works Section
Every strong competitor shows a simple 3-4 step process. This removes friction and builds trust. Visitors want to know: "What happens after I reach out?" Show: Consultation → Design → Install → Enjoy.

### Pattern 3: Categorized Project Gallery with High-Quality Photos
The #1 decision driver for interior design clients is seeing past work. Best-in-class sites organize by room type (Bedroom, Living Room, Office) and use large, full-bleed photography. This is where the Instagram auto-sync is a game-changer.

### Pattern 4: Social Proof Prominently Placed
Testimonials near the top, not buried in the footer. Google review stars, client photos, before/after comparisons. AmiSa has a testimonials page — they should be shown on every key page.

### Pattern 5: Fast, Friction-Free Contact
One click to call, one click to get a quote. Best sites have a sticky CTA button on mobile ("Get Free Quote"), a simple form (name, phone, room type), and a fast response promise. The biggest gap for all competitors in this market.

## Competitive Opportunity Summary

The Houston accent wall market has **weak competition** — most sites are template-based with poor photography and no clear differentiation. AmiSa has a real opportunity to be the premium, design-forward option with:

1. A beautiful, scroll-animated website (none of the competitors have this)
2. A live Instagram gallery that auto-updates (Instagram proof = trust)
3. Room-type gallery filtering (Bedroom / Living Room / Office / Kids Room)
4. A clear "How It Works" process section
5. Prominent testimonials and social proof
6. Mobile-first design with instant "Get Quote" CTA
