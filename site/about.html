The Artist

Meet the
Mind Behind AmiSa

At AmiSa, every wall tells a story — and it starts with yours. I founded AmiSa with a simple belief: your home should feel like you. Not a catalog. Not a template. Your unique vision brought to life on the walls you live with every day.

Based in Houston, TX, I specialize in custom accent wall designs and interior wall painting with a focus on precision, creativity, and craftsmanship. From bold geometric patterns to soft, textured finishes — every project is designed from scratch, tailored to your space.

When you work with AmiSa, you're not working with a contractor — you're working with an artist who genuinely loves what they do. I take pride in every brushstroke, every line, every finished room.
